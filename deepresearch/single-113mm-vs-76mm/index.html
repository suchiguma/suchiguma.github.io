Deep Mechanics

Vanguard guns operate at a frequency of 10 Hertz That is they attempt to fire 10 times per second A gun that reloads in 0 45s and the same gun that reloads in 0 47s are identical in reality because they are both rounded up to 0 50s Meanwhile a gun that reloads in 0 50s is unpredictable because it can go at 0 50s or 0 60s randomly depending on device lag Other factors that affect vanguard firing rate such as animation trigger time are outside the scope of this article From a pure minmaxing perspective the only advantage of the Blue 76 over Gold 113 is its reload time Everything else is worse Hence Blue 76 only has a chance to beat Gold 113 if it crosses a 10 Hertz threshold In most maxed out destroyers cases that threshold is 0 50s In the following tests we explored different reload values on the ship to see whether Blue 76 can beat Gold 113 at certain reload values
