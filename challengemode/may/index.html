Strategy

This is a straightforward but somewhat difficult DPS check Takao s Steel Blade Stance henceforth SBS with 90% damage reduction lasts until you deal 20 000 damage to her Effectively this means if your fleet would have dealt 300 000 damage within 10s then 200 000 of that damage becomes 20 000 and you are left with 120 000 damage dealt to Takao Viewed from a different angle if your fleet interrupts Takao 29 times over 10 minutes then an effective 29 * 180 000 5 220 000 HP is added to Takao s base 5 230 000 HP You must have a well synced main fleet that interrupts Takao s damage reduction and hits her within 10s in every reload cycle Also your vanguard takes damage faster as the battle goes on so if you need 9 or 10 minutes for your DPS level then you will need a CB or Anchorage level tank And that s it if your account is strong then you can brute force this month If not then there are some small optimizations to augment your DPS against SBS phases Takao s 90% damage reduction is coded as her taking 90% damage from each of FP based TRP based and AVI based damage This damage reduction does not affect damage over time effects like burn and flood Effectively burns and floods do 10x as much damage while Takao is in SBS Hypothetically if you were to use TRP as your main source of damage for some reason you might want to bring Noshiro or Tanikaze Matchless They make the boss take 40% damage from TRP based damage and 90% 40% 50% Going from 10% to 50% damage is a huge 5x increase in damage for the first 20 000 damage and a 1 4x increase for any extra damage after that But nobody will use TRP as the main damage source Fortunately FP and AVI also have some ship skills that are additive with the 90% albeit not as crazy as 40% Here is a list of recommended bonuses to consider against Takao Most burns last for 15s so you may give Mk 7 to the second or third BB to fire preferably not the first Most armor breaks give 8% to FP based damage effectively 1 8x damage to SBS Guam and gir are natural candidates for BB fleets Scylla gives 6% to AVI based damage effectively 1 6x damage to SBS Make sure her torpedo reloads slightly faster than the CVs Floods There are not many ships who inflict flooding and the good ships among them are even fewer

Manual

This section is for manual only and only months with a notable manual strategy may have a manual section Use carriers with a freeze like Implacable or August of course and wait for 40 seconds until each carrier has two airstrikes ready Dump them all at once Repeat This strategy effectively halves the number of 90% damage SBS you have to deal with increasing your damage per minute

Mid-High End BB

This fleet won in 8 49 with mid cats half tech and no subs Like CVs BBs are quite bad without a slow or stop But CVs have August von Parseval while BBs currently have no permanent option that s reliable So we assume you have at least one of Bismarck Zwei Eldridge Retrofit or Sovetsky Soyuz In this example Soyuz has the best gun damage and takes the UR SAP gun making her the slowest of the BBs This setup is quite convenient as we can give a single BB both SAP and AFCT to boost her damage but don t try to force AFCT with SAP if it means using worse BBs Vanguard must not be more than 1 2s faster than Soyuz Giving her the Champagne gun is right around 1 2s so it ll probably be fine but using Soyuz gun if you have it is a safe play Mid High End BB example fleet

Mid-End CV

This fleet won in 9 29 with mid cats half tech and no subs If you use Scylla make sure her torpedoes reload slightly faster or as fast as the fastest CV In this example using 41 CV RLD after cats Fritz is 20 96s and Scylla with 13 CL RLD is 20 87s Unfortunately quad mag torpedoes would reload too fast even with 0 CL RLD and the Crossbow augment Franklin is a nice sidegrade to Enterprise and even has a flood But in a 10 minute battle she will burn herself for over 5000 HP and Takao can also blind fire Franklin if your whole main fleet is concealed So don t use Franklin unless you also use Kearsarge Mid End CV example fleet
