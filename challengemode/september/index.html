Manual

This section is for manual only and only months with a notable manual strategy may have a manual section With CVs it is easy just dump airstrikes outside the 10s window of 40% EVA RATE With BBs you probably should still time the DDG missiles but you can supplement the TRP damage by launching manual torpedoes at the right time You can also hold the BB salvos so that they don t hit during the 10s of 40% EVA RATE
