Strategy

Heavy armor HP piatas If you sink both bosses within 8s of each other then the DPS check is quite easy Otherwise they can heal a lot making the DPS check harder but still brute force able Note This month can share the same brute force fleets as March so look there if you hope for slightly different options to copy

Manual

This section is for manual only and only months with a notable manual strategy may have a manual section At the cost of vanguard survivability you can use your vanguard to ram one boss to slow her down Keep ramming until the two bosses are stacked together Your main fleet s DPS will be greatly increased due to splash yes all kinds of torpedoes do have small splash Do not bring single target slows like Eldridge or Aegir because they can separate the bosses

Mid-End CV

This fleet won in 8 28 with 20% tech mid cats and no submarines Kazagumo sank after 7 minutes The carriers reload times must be quite close and August von Parseval needs to be the second or third carrier to strike A sync within 0 06s on the calculator should be perfect for 3 minutes and then begin degrading Try to find a sync within 0 03s For no oaths and between 28 and 46 CV RLD from cats and tech this gear has a really good sync Calculator link Mid End CV example fleet

Advanced CV

This fleet won in 3 59 with oaths half tech mid cats and no submarines The bosses healed the maximum of three times Bismarck Zwei pulls the bosses together and CV torpedoes can often splash both bosses The timing is very strict For an unoathed main fleet this works with 54 CV RLD and 0 BB RLD For oathed it s 56 CV and 0 BB If you re missing 2 or 3 CV RLD swap Bishamaru to the staff cat position although this will separate Shinano and Amagi a bit more For even more damage adjust Unzen s torpedo reload time to be the same as the main fleet s reload time Then she would both hit more and splash two bosses more Calculator link unoathed Calculator link oathed Advanced CV example fleet
