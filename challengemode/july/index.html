Strategy

Prevent Healing Every 27s Chelae the claw attempts to grab your vanguard If it is successful and survives for 9s Spee heals for 5% max HP There are two ways to avoid Spee s healing There is a 1 2s delay between the claw s aim and the claw s grab Using a fast vanguard greatly increases the chance of the claw missing Use BBs secondary guns to kill the claw within 9s This is quite difficult and inconsistent even with 3 BBs For example the vanguard and claw could be too far down or right for the top BB to hit it You will also need high vanguard DPS to help The claw being a real enemy increases your CV detection gauge when it is to the left of the blue detection line please turn on the Display CV Detection Line setting if you haven t already If you use one or more CVs it is highly important to use a fast vanguard or manual Tanking Warning Spee does a lot of damage to both vanguard and main fleet Spee regularly attacks a revealed ship in the main fleet Your flagship is most likely to be the target if she is revealed She can die in 4 to 6 minutes without survival gear If you don t have a strong fleet to win fast then give your BB a washing machine if the other 2 are concealed carriers or give the same to your flagship if you use multiple BBs Using 3 carriers is not safe from the above attack due to carriers naturally increasing detection from airstrikes or barrages Furthermore Spee regularly fires a burning barrage like many other CM bosses if the whole main fleet is concealed Scepter is a must and a washing machine on the carrier who is most likely to be revealed may be advisable For the vanguard bring a real main tank while keeping speed high with Beaver Badge Boilers based Boiler Cosmic Kicks and or better Cosmic Kicks
