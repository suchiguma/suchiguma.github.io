Ship Gain Worth New
SKK		 Notes
Akane Shinjo
Confinement Program
Adds ~22% damage because it more than doubles her barrage and adds a summon.
Albacore
Prank-of-the-Day!
Basically quadruples her barrages, but equipped torpedos are still the main source of damage.
Amity
Crown of Flowing Flames
Better debuffing, which is Amity's whole point.
Ayanami
Type-35 "Ayanami" Anti-Ship Armament
Very strong augment for manual players, but check if you have her side/upgrades like Jintsuu META, Ibuki, or Shimakaze.
BLACK★ROCK SHOOTER
Rock Cannon
Very good stats, +30% chance on a strong barrage, adds a weak barrage.
Centaur
Sylvan Dawnbreeze
About 20% more damage if there are 3 HMS ships, dropping to 9% more if not.
Homura
Six Swords - Homura the Pioneer
Effectively increased OXY, 20% more crit dmg, and a broken strong barrage to make one of the best submarines.
Hood
Dame's Medal of Grace
Adds ~29% L, ~11% M/H damage. Barrage 100% chance and 2x light dmg, making her a solid choice for new players.
Ibuki
Blade of the Holy Mountain
Strong barrage with no CD makes Ibuki a top torp ship, but the barrage is long and there are few CA torp buffers.
Lala Satalin Deviluke
Omnitool
Doubles an ok barrage, adds another ok barrage, and adds nice stats.
Momo Belia Deviluke
Burst Flower
More flower = block more suicide boats. Also add ok stats and ok laser.
Neptune
Neptune's Trident
Huge buffs to both damage and survivability, making Neptune decent.
Roon
Purse of Everyday Items
Augment is huge, overall like +50% damage. Now Roon is top gold CA level like a less tanky Drake with more damage.
Shoukaku
Call of a Thousand Cranes
Adds 54% dmg if solo, or 30% dmg if comparing augment+Zuikaku with Zuikaku. So it reduces her reliance on Zuikaku.
Tashkent
The Ice Sprite's Kindness
Removes the main gun lock and greatly improves her damage. New players also benefit a lot when lacking good guns.
U-101
"Great Shark"
Adds 15-25% more damage overall (hard to calculate the movement change), making U-101 better than U-37 and U-47.
U-96
Special Mix (Do Not Drink)
Adds ~14% damage if off flag, and now U-96 is strong enough to be put in flag for her barrage. Good for wolfpack.
Yumia Liessfeldt
Ephemeral Record
Has very strong stats (40 AVI, 20 HIT), brings Item Effects from 10s to 8s, and adds a decent barrage on airstrike.
