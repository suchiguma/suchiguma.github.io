Hull		 Augment Notes
DDDual SwordsUse this by default if you don't know. Good for most DDs since they have low base FP stat. Slash clears torpedos.
DDHammerGood for DDs that scale heavily on TRP stat. Slash clears bullets. Sometimes good for reaching Level 3 Spread Slash for armor break.
CLSwordSlightly better defense than Crossbow due to bullet-clearing slash. Sometimes good for reaching Level 3 Spread Slash for armor break.
CLCrossbowSlightly better offense than Sword due to FP stat. Bring this for Chapter 13 and 15.
CAGreatswordUse this by default if you don't know. Better defense than Lance due to torpedo-clearing slash (and CA/CBs are usually weak to torpedos).
CALanceSometimes slightly better offense than Greatsword against bosses with medium to high evasion.
BBOfficer's SwordUse this by default if you don't know. Good everywhere due to HIT stat, and especially good when enemy BBs shoot your BBs.
BBBowgunGood against heavy armor bosses or bosses with very low evasion.
CVScepterUse this by default if you don't know. Especially good when enemies target or blind-fire your CVs.
CVHunting BowUse this in Chapter 13 and 15.
SSDaggerUse this. Torpedo crit rate is worth more than Kunai's TRP stat.
SSKunaiDon't use this. Worse than Dagger.
