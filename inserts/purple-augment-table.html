Hull		 Augment Notes
DD
Dual Swords
Use this by default if you don't know. Good for most DDs since they have low base FP stat. Slash clears torpedos.
DD
Hammer
Good for DDs that scale heavily on TRP stat. Slash clears bullets. Sometimes good for reaching Level 3 Spread Slash for armor break.
CL
Sword
Slightly better defense than Crossbow due to bullet-clearing slash. Sometimes good for reaching Level 3 Spread Slash for armor break.
CL
Crossbow
Slightly better offense than Sword due to FP stat. Bring this for Chapter 13 and 15.
CA
Greatsword
Use this by default if you don't know. Better defense than Lance due to torpedo-clearing slash (and CA/CBs are usually weak to torpedos).
CA
Lance
Sometimes slightly better offense than Greatsword against bosses with medium to high evasion.
BB
Officer's Sword
Use this by default if you don't know. Good everywhere due to HIT stat, and especially good when enemy BBs shoot your BBs.
BB
Bowgun
Good against heavy armor bosses or bosses with very low evasion.
CV
Scepter
Use this by default if you don't know. Especially good when enemies target or blind-fire your CVs.
CV
Hunting Bow
Use this in Chapter 13 and 15.
SS
Dagger
Use this. Torpedo crit rate is worth more than Kunai's TRP stat.
SS
Kunai
Don't use this. Worse than Dagger.
