Introduction

This mini guide is intended to provide the most complete information for new players to select their research focus although older players may learn something new too We list the advantages and disadvantages of focusing on each season to help you make an informed decision Alternatively you may follow the overall recommendation directly See ECGC s Research Guide for other aspects of research The advice in our mini guide overrides the Research Focus section We also strongly recommend analyzing all Combat Data Packs and recommend implementing the PR Catch up Min Maxing section on that guide into your gameplay

Overall Recommendation

Select PR DR 7 or PR DR 8 as your Research Focus Season 7 s gold ships and gear are much stronger while Season 8 has more flexible rainbows and provides the best long term value One of Season 7 s rainbow ships has great reliance on Sovetsky Soyuz so if you will not be able to obtain and max Soyuz within the next 5 or so months then Season 8 may provide better value Note Soyuz is expected to rerun around March 2026 but you also need to spare some UR Bulins for her which may or may not be possible depending on how good the 6 new URs per year are
