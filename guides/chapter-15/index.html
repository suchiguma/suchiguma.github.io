Gear Notes

If you haven t been playing Operation Siren you probably won t win in Chapter 15 Lots of the best gear is in Gear Lab and 13 gear is a huge boost in strength Bring Hedgehog and at least one other ASW weapon on the mob fleet for 15 3 and 15 4 Options include a Fairey Gannet or TBM Avenger VT 51 on a CVL a purple depth charge or Eldridge Retrofit Auxes standard auxes so cruisers all use Rudder But cruisers especially the middle slot can also use Anti Torpedo Bulge as the other aux if they are not occupied with ASW Mob fleet BB wants 457mm Twin 410 kai for Musashi Boss fleet BB can use another 457mm or a 20s AP triple gun like champ gun The other gear are quite standard see Quick Gear Guide and look for mob or campaign in each slot type
